Make Saving Fun

An idea to attract young people into saving more by turning arcade machine into a coin deposit machine

Background

This is my school project back in NAFA. The brief was for Frank OCBC to help youth in their saving habit. It's one of my favorite project. Aside from winning an award surprisingly (Advertising, Silver, Crowbar Award 2015), I think this work speaks for myself as a designer to bring out delightful experience.

My role

Student

The problem & approach

The Problem

Low rate of saving habit in youth

Brainstorming

Always start with the subject when trying to solve a problem, first few attempts came up with all the basic ideas that solve the main problem, but here we need something that engaging and relates with the target audience.

About target audience

High spending habit
Low saving habit
Spend most of the times in shopping mall or friend's house
Spend mostly on foods and entertainment

Solution

Insights

Youngsters just like to have fun, so we're gonna make saving fun too for them.

Ideas

While spending their money, they left with coins which they don't like to keep. We create a fun place where they can get rid off their coins from the pocket to save it in their bank account.

Solution

Arcade/Coin-deposit machine
